MS 2750/416/55

A. R. Moon, Esq.,
46, Redhill Drive,
EDGWARE. April 20th, 1937
Dear Sir,
We thank you for your letter of 
April 17th, and are pleased to give you permission
to quote the sentence you mention from THE
EDWARDIANS in your forthcoming text-book on English.
We shall be grateful if you will 
make due acknowledgments to the author and to our-
selves as publishers, as you suggest.
Yours Faithfully,
THE HOGARTH PRESS,
MANAGER.
